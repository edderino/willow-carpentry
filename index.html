FAQ

Before you enquire

A few basics before sending through the job.

What type of work does Willow take on?

Decks, pergolas, outdoor living areas, residential carpentry, exterior timber details and selected renovation work.

Do I need plans before getting in touch?

Not always. For smaller carpentry work, photos and a short description may be enough to start the conversation. Larger work may need more detail before quoting.

Can you quote from photos?

Photos are useful for the first look, but most jobs still need a proper site check before a final quote.

Do you work across Canberra?

Yes, Willow works across Canberra and nearby suburbs depending on the job and schedule.

Do you do small jobs?

Some smaller carpentry jobs are a good fit. Send the details through and Willow can let you know whether it suits the schedule and scope.

What should I include in an enquiry?

Your suburb, the type of work, rough timing, a short description and any photos of the area are the most useful starting points.
