Decks

Custom decks built to make outdoor areas more usable, practical and enjoyable.

Willow Carpentry & Construction builds decks for Canberra homes, whether it is a new outdoor area, a replacement for an old deck, or an extension of an existing space.

The focus is on making the deck suit the home, the access points and the way the space will actually be used.

What this includes

  • New deck builds
  • Deck replacements
  • Deck extensions
  • Outdoor entertaining areas
  • Steps and access points
  • Screening and finishing details
  • Handrails or balustrade details where required
